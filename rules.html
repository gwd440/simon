ALMOST SIMON

RULES
  1. Click Start to Begin
  2. When you hear / see the first signal, repeat the signal by clicking on the same colour panel
  3. The first signal will be duplicated and a new one added. Repeat these two signals by clicking on the same colour panels, in order.
  4. The first two signals will be duplicated and another signal added.
  5. Continue playing as long as you can repeat each sequence of signals correctly.
  6. After the 5th, 9th and 13th signals in a sequence, the signals speed up.
  7. If you fail to repeat a sequence exactly, or if you take more than 5 seconds to repeat a signal, Simon responds with a “RAZZ” sound. This means you've lost and the sequence of signals ends.
  8. How to win: Repeat the number of sequences 20 times and be rewarded with 6 short signals from the last panel you clicked on.
