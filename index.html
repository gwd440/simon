ALMOST SIMON

Almost Simon is based on the original Simon electronic game, first developed in the 1970s.

The original game device created a series of tones and light and required the player to repeat the sequence.

As the player succeeded, the series became progressively longer and more complex.

If the player made a mistake in the sequence, the game was over.
